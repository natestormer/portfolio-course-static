Hello,
I’m John S. Sargent

Leading portraint painter of a generation.

An American expatriate portrait and landscape painter living in London, England.

Let's Work Together

Prior to opening my studio in London, I studied in Paris, France under Carolus-Duran, James Carroll Beckwith, and Léon Bonnat. I specialize in portrait, landscape and en plein air works executed in oils, watercolor and charcoal.

My body of work has drawn much acclaim for my evocations of Edwardian-era luxury
